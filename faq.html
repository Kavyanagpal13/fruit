Frequently Asked Questions

What are the health benefits of Tangerines?

Tangerines are rich in vitamin C, which helps boost the immune system and improve skin health. They also contain antioxidants and fiber.

How should I store Tangerines?

Tangerines should be stored in a cool, dry place or in the refrigerator. They stay fresh longer when kept in a crisper drawer.

Can Tangerines help with digestion?

Yes, Tangerines are high in fiber, which aids digestion and helps maintain a healthy gut.

Are there any potential drawbacks to eating too many Tangerines?

While Tangerines are healthy, consuming them in excess may lead to excessive fiber intake, which can cause digestive discomfort. Moderation is key.
